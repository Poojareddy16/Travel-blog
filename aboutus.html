About Wanderlust Chronicles

Wanderlust Chroniclesis an adventure travel blog by Olly Gaspar featuring free and helpful travel guides and inspiring photography from some of the world’s most epic destinations. From waterfall hikes to ocean sailing, We Seek Travel gives readers the information and inspiration they need to plan an awesome adventure & explore more on every trip.

Hey Friends! My Name is Pooja.

I’m an indefinite traveler, and many know me as a nomad, tour guide, photographer, blogger, adventurer, festival fairy, solo traveler, serial experience seeker, and/or self-proclaimed mermaid

I’m passionate about making the most of life – obsessive, some may say – which is why I started this blog to help you do the same! Here I share my tips from living, working, traveling, and struggling

Wanderlust Chroniclesis designed to inspire young-at-heart travelers to plan AND make the most of a dream trip. I focus on unique + boutique travel experiences perfect for the insta-savvy adventurer who enjoys going off the beaten track while finding the best photo ops and cute spots – on a budget but with the occasional splurge.

I love being in nature – I love long walks through the forest, a hike up an island or a stroll down a beach. I love as much exploring the tiny unknown corners in a city.

Photography is a big part of our travels and we make sure we have enough time to spend on making good pictures.
